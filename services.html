Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a complex application could take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our discovery phase.

What is your pricing model?

We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials pricing models. For most projects, we recommend fixed-price for better budget predictability. We'll discuss the best option for your needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Absolutely! We offer various maintenance and support packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. Our team is available 24/7 for critical issues.

Can you work with our existing team?

Yes! We frequently collaborate with in-house teams. Whether you need full project delivery or team augmentation, we adapt our approach to fit your needs.
